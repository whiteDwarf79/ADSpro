Uploading Files:

- Click on the Get Started button
- The ADsPro interface will appear
- Upload an Audio(take audio containing only one sentence at a time for better accuracy)
- Use the "Tab" key to play/pause the audio during playback.
- Upload a TextGrid file(single tier praat generated .textgrid extension files are currently supported) see "help!" button for more details
- use their respective buttons for upload.

Analyzing Audio:

- Once both audio and TextGrid files are uploaded, you'll see the waveform visualization of the canvas display.
- Below the canvas the word blocks(interval) will appear according to the uploaded TextGrid file of praat.

Checking Prominence:

- Select the block of word(interval) and Single click the "Check prominence here!" button to analyze the audio segments and predict word prominence.
- Prominent words will be highlighted in red, while non-prominent words will be displayed in grey.

Additional Features:

- The application is designed to be responsive, allowing access from various devices.
