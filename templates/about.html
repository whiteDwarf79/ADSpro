Welcome to ADsPro!

ADsPro is a simple tool to help you analyze audio files and find the important words in them. It's easy to use and helps you understand the details of any audio.

Our Mission

We want to make it easy for everyone, from researchers to hobbyists, to work with audio data.

What We Offer

  • Audio Analysis: Quickly upload audio files and learn about their features.
  • TextGrid Integration: Use TextGrid files to see and study parts of the audio.
  • Prominence Prediction: Find out which words are most important in your audio.

Why Choose ADsPro?

  • Accuracy: Get reliable audio analysis results.
  • Ease of Use: Our platform is designed for everyone, no special skills needed.
  • Versatility: Great for research, learning languages, and more.
